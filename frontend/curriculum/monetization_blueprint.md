MODULE 1 — Understanding Business Problems
Objective
Teach students to think beyond tools.
Businesses do NOT buy:
Power BI,
SQL,
dashboards.
Businesses buy:
visibility,
profit clarity,
automation,
cost reduction,
operational control.

Teach This Core Principle
WRONG POSITIONING
“I am a data analyst.”
BETTER POSITIONING
“I help businesses track performance and automate reporting.”

MODULE 5 — Pitching SMEs
Objective
Teach sales communication.

Structure of a Good SME Pitch
Step 1 — Identify Pain
“Do you struggle tracking sales clearly?”
Step 2 — Explain Cost
“Manual reports waste time and hide losses.”
Step 3 — Present Solution
“I can automate this into a simple dashboard.”
Step 4 — Show Result
“You’ll track performance instantly.”

Important Teaching Point
Avoid technical jargon.

BAD PITCH
“I use Power BI and SQL for visualization.”
GOOD PITCH
“I help businesses know where money is going.”

Assignment
Students practice:
60-second elevator pitch
physical business pitch
WhatsApp pitch

MODULE 6 — Discovery Meetings
Objective
Teach requirement gathering.

What Students Must Learn
Before building anything:

What reports do you check most?
What takes the most time?
Operations

Which reports are manual?

MODULE 7 — Proposal Writing
Objective
Teach professional deal closing.

Proposal Structure
1. Introduction
Who you are.
2. Client Problem
Explain their challenge.
3. Proposed Solution
Dashboard/reporting system.
4. Deliverables
Exactly what they receive.
5. Timeline
Expected completion.
6. Pricing
Cost breakdown.
7. Payment Terms
Deposit requirements.

Sample Payment Terms

30% after completion

Important Rule
Never start major work without upfront payment.
